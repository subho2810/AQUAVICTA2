Water Treatment Chemicals

  • We recommend specialty chemicals for cooling water and boiler water treatment after doing thorough study of the water systems. After we study the plant data and the makeup water data we design chemical treatment program.
  • Cooling water treatment is done for the inhibition of corrosion, scaling, fouling and microbio fouling.
  • Boiler water treatment is done to prevent corrosion and scaling inside the boiler.
  • We supply and apply the chemicals for cooling water and boiler water. Regular monitoring of the water system is also done in order to keep a check on the health of the water system and also understand the efficiency of the treatment program
